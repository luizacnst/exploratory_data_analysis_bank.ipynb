Credit Card Customer Churn Analysis
Business Problem
The company's management has noticed a significant increase in the number of customers canceling their credit cards. This growing churn rate is causing substantial financial losses and raises concerns about customer retention.

Understanding the factors that lead customers to cancel their credit cards is essential for developing strategies to reduce churn and improve customer loyalty.

Objective
The goal of this analysis is to explore the available customer data and identify patterns associated with credit card cancellations. By analyzing customer behavior and characteristics, we aim to understand which factors may contribute to churn and which customers are more likely to cancel their credit cards.

Dataset
The dataset contains information about bank customers, including both active clients and those who have canceled their credit cards. These data will be used to perform exploratory data analysis and identify potential patterns related to customer churn.

Tools Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib / Seaborn
